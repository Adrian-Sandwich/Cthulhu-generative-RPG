## Key Observations

### What Works Excellently ✅

1. **Character Differentiation** - Each investigator has distinct playstyle
2. **Skill System** - D100 rolls work properly with difficulty modifiers
3. **Sanity Mechanics** - Conditional damage system functioning
4. **Atmosphere** - Horror tone maintained throughout
5. **State Tracking** - HP/SAN/Turn count accurate
6. **Natural Language** - Players can describe actions in plain English

### What Needs Improvement ⚠️

1. **Ending Triggers** - Need more defined conditions for players to reach conclusions
2. **NPC Interaction** - Can't talk to locals or NPCs, only monologue
3. **Inventory** - No picking up items or managing equipment
4. **World Progression** - Time doesn't progress, world state static
5. **Failure Consequences** - HP damage not implemented yet
6. **Multiple Enemies** - Can't fight or encounter multiple creatures

### Interesting Patterns 📊

- **Military characters** (Redford) lose less sanity due to training
- **Aggressive investigation** (Chen, Morgan #9) leads to faster sanity damage
- **Retreat strategy** (Morgan #5) minimizes horror exposure
- **Faith-based** (Sister) can succeed at unique checks but have dramatic failures
- **Critical failures** are more impactful than expected (Sister turn 3: -25 SAN!)
